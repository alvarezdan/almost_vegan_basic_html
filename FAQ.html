What is the difference between vegan and vegetarian?

Veganism is the practice of abstaining from the use of all animal products. Vegetarian diets do not utilize meat, and sometimes other animal products. For example, honey comes from an animal so it is not vegan but since it is not meat a vegetarian might consume it.
